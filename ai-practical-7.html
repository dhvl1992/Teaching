🧠 How the AI Learns Relationships

During training, the AI saw patterns like this millions of times:

Pattern: "The [BUILDING] is [NUMBER] [UNIT] tall"
Examples: "The tower is 330 meters tall", "The building is 100 feet tall"
Pattern: "How [HEIGHT_WORD] is the [OBJECT]?"
Examples: "How tall is the tower?", "How high is the mountain?"

The AI learned that questions about height expect measurement answers!
