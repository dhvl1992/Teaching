Evolution of Management Thought

1880s-1911

Taylor's Scientific Management

Focus on individual worker efficiency through scientific methods. Time and motion studies, differential payment systems. Applied today in manufacturing and operations.

1916

Fayol's Administrative Theory

Management as universal process - POCCC activities and 14 principles. Focus on management functions rather than just worker productivity. Foundation of modern management education.

1950s-60s

Systems Approach

Organizations as open systems interacting with environment. Holistic view considering all stakeholders. Applied in complexity management and digital transformation.

Modern Era

Indian Applications

Successful adaptation in ISRO (scientific management), Tata Group (Fayol's principles), Flipkart (systems approach). Demonstrates universal relevance of classical theories.
